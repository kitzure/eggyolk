how to use the bookmarklet

copy this bookmarklet

create a new browser bookmark and paste this into the URL field. it works on any VTC page.

tap to zoom in for tutorial

steps

1

log in to the VTC portal

go to myportal.vtc.edu.hk and make sure you're logged in.

2

click the bookmark

a dark status card pops up. it fetches your calendar lessons in the background, then finds your attendance record page and grabs your present / late / absent status for every module.

3

tracker opens automatically

the attendance tracker opens and immediately shows rates for all modules. thats all.
