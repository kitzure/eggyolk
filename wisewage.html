How to use wisewage

1
Set your profile
Go to the profile tab and add your name, monthly salary, payday date, work hours, and current bank balance. This is the foundation for all calculations.
2
Add your expenses
On the dashboard, enter the amount and description (e.g., "lunch", "transport") and click "add expense". Your balance will update automatically.
3
View your stats
Check the stats tab to see your salary breakdown, daily spending charts, MPF contributions, and balance trends over time.
4
Tips & tricks
- The safe daily budget shows how much you can spend per day until payday.
- Use the AI chat for budget advice.
- Export your data regularly as backup.
